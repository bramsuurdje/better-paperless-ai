# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Paperless users managing a personal or small-business document archive. They need to review OCR output, classify incoming documents, and find records without working directly in Paperless's administration interface.

## Product Purpose

Make document classification fast and trustworthy by combining Paperless metadata with reviewable AI suggestions. Success means users can clear an unclassified inbox quickly while retaining control over every destructive or metadata-changing action.

## Brand Personality

Calm, efficient, and precise. The interface should feel dependable and self-explanatory rather than clever or decorative.

## Anti-references

Avoid cramped admin tables, ambiguous raw-value controls, decorative AI-dashboard styling, and workflows that hide consequential changes behind automation.

## Design Principles

- Make the current scope and filter state obvious.
- Keep high-frequency document actions close to the document.
- Use AI as a reviewable assistant, not an opaque authority.
- Separate related controls clearly and give dense information enough breathing room.
- Make destructive actions deliberate and reversible where Paperless permits it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA with keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, semantic labels, sufficient contrast, reduced-motion support, and no reliance on color alone.
